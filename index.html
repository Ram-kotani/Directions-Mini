Directions from Riga

Ilūkste is located approximately 200 kilometers southeast of Riga. Here are the directions and transportation options:

Unfortunately there's no airport or airservices to ilukste. the nearest airport is in Riga(capital city) where you can look out some amazing places in riga like old town and museums.

You can find information to Explore Riga .

1. By Train

  • No direct trains run to Ilūkste, but you can take a train to Daugavpils and then take a local bus or taxi to Ilūkste.
  • Train tickets can be booked at Pasažieru Vilciens (Latvian Railways).

2. By Bus

  • Direct buses run from Riga to Ilūkste. The journey takes about 3-4 hours.
  • Tickets can be purchased online at Riga International Bus Terminal.

3. By Car

  • Take the A6/E22 highway from Riga towards Daugavpils, and then follow local roads to Ilūkste.
  • The drive takes approximately 2.5-3 hours, depending on traffic.

4. By Bicycle

  • Cycling from Riga to Ilūkste is a long journey of approximately 200 kilometers. Plan your route carefully using bike-friendly roads.
  • You can use tools like Komoot or Ride with GPS for planning.
